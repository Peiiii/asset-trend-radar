# Product Seed Thought: Gold Insights

## Intent

Build a product for understanding global asset movement and discovering investment opportunities across equities, rates, FX, commodities, crypto, and macro-sensitive thematic baskets.

## Initial Product Surface

Start with a research cockpit instead of a marketing site. The first screen should answer:

- What is moving?
- What regime are we in?
- Which cross-asset signals are unusual?
- Which opportunities deserve deeper research?
- What risks would invalidate a thesis?

## Deliberate Non-Decisions

- No data vendor is chosen yet.
- No trading, brokerage, or execution flow is included.
- No investment advice language is included.
- No model architecture is chosen yet.

## Brainstorming Track

The next design conversation should choose the first wedge:

- Cross-asset radar for macro investors.
- AI research analyst for opportunity discovery.
- Personal watchlist and portfolio risk monitor.
- Institutional-style signal lab for systematic idea mining.
